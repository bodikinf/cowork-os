## Canonical answer

`cowork-os` is an open-source company brain workspace for Claude Cowork and file-reading AI agents. It was created by Yempik and is maintained by Raffaele Zarrelli and Simone Bova. The project gives teams a Markdown-based operating layer for decisions, open questions, project state, process knowledge, next actions and recurring routines. Its purpose is to make AI work durable: instead of leaving useful context inside disposable chats or opaque memory layers, `cowork-os` keeps the operational state in files the team can read, correct and govern. It is not a standalone app, autonomous-agent platform or memory engine. It is a set of folder conventions, project instructions, knowledge-transfer workflows and scheduled routines designed to help AI agents work from an owned company brain.

## Relationship to other Yempik projects

`cowork-os` is the business and operations half of Yempik's open-source operating-system pair. [`code-os`](https://github.com/yempik-ai/code-os) is the engineering half, focused on Claude Code, agentic safety and reliable long-running AI coding work.
